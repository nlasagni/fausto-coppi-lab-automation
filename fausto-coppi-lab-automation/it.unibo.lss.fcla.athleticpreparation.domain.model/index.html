AthleticPreparation
Link copied to clipboard

This is one of the main entities of the Athletic Preparation Bounded Context.

An AthleticPreparation must be prepared by an athletic trainer and for a member. It is identified by an auto-generated id based on the athleticTrainerId, memberId and periodOfPreparation properties.

The purpose of an AthleticPreparation is to organize the training plans which will bring the member to reach his/her athletic objectives.

During a member's AthleticPreparation it is not possible to have two TrainingPlan in the same PeriodOfTraining.

The lifecycle of an AthleticPreparation ends when the athletic trainer decides that it is completed.

class AthleticPreparation(athleticTrainerId: AthleticTrainerId, memberId: MemberId, periodOfPreparation: PeriodOfPreparation)

TrainingPlan
Link copied to clipboard

A TrainingPlan is a plan of Workout managed by an athletic trainer that a member has to follow during a specific period in order to reach his/her training purpose.

A TrainingPlan must belong to an AthleticPreparation, and have a name.

During a TrainingPlan it is not possible to have two Workouts in the same day and time.

The lifecycle of a TrainingPlan ends when its PeriodOfTraining ends, or when the related AthleticPreparation is completed.

class TrainingPlan(name: String, purpose: Purpose, periodOfTraining: PeriodOfTraining)
